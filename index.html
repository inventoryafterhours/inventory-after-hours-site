What’s in Your Inventory Report?

Our inventory reports are detailed, impartial, and fully compliant with tenancy deposit protection requirements. Each report includes:

  • ✅ Cover sheet with inspection date, address, and inspector details
  • 🏡 Room-by-room breakdown of furniture, fixtures, fittings, walls, floors, windows, and appliances
  • 📸 High-quality photographs documenting the condition of items and surfaces
  • 📌 Notes on cleanliness, damage, missing items, and wear & tear
  • 📦 Notes on meter readings, keys provided, smoke detector checks
  • 🖊️ Signature page to be signed by both the tenant(s) and landlord or agent
  • 📄 Delivered in PDF format, typically within 72–96 hours (or 24 hours with express add-on)

Reports are suitable for check-in, check-out, and interim inspections. They are formatted for ease of comparison over the course of a tenancy and serve as a vital reference for deposit-related discussions.
